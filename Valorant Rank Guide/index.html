Iron

The things to work on iron to move on to bronze can be summed up as :-

  • Getting to know the objectives of the game
  • Getting to know the different abilities of different agents and chose one as your main.
  • Learning about the different guns in the game and chose one as your main.
  • Not constantly looking at the ground.[Crosshair Placement]

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________

Bronze

The things to work on bronze to move on to silver can be summed up as :-

  • Understanding how the credits should be used based on rounds.
  • Diving deeper into the agent you have decided to main.
  • Learning about the spray patterns of different guns, and trying to not hold crouch in every fight.
  • Crosshair Placement.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________

Silver

The things to work on silver to move on to gold can be summed up as :-

  • Thinking more about the duels you take and where it will put your team if you are to lose.
  • Looking more at the mini map and giving callouts to your teammates.
  • Trying to spray as little as possible and mostly burst to kill enemies instead. (Unless the fight is very close ranged.)
  • Finding the perfect sensetivity for yourself if you havent found one yet.
  • Crosshair Placement.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________

Gold

The things to work on gold to move on to plat can be summed up as :-

  • Warming up in range/aimtrainers/deathmatches before jumping into compititive.
  • Positioning.
  • Movement (Strafing / Peeking)
  • Having a good mental and not getting tilted.
  • Crosshair Placement
